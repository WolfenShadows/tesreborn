OOC Basics

  1. Treat others as they wish to be treated, this is the Platinum Rule.
  2. When in the RP channel, try to keep OOC chatter to a minimum, if you must use OOC, please use double parenthesis.
    • DO NOT use any other OOC signifier, as our chat parser only accepts double parenthesis.

Character Creation

  1. Read all instructions before starting character creation.
  2. You may not begin play until after your character has been approved.
